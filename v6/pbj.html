How to Make a Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich

Ever wanted to make a quality PB&J sandwich, but suddenly realized you've forgotten how to do it? Don't worry â€“ we all have those days. Here is a comprehensive guide to help you make that lunchtime classic.

Ingredients

  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly
  • 2 slices of bread

Directions

  1. For a crispier sandwich, toast the bread first
    1. Place both slices of bread in a toaster or toaster oven
    2. Activate the toasting element
    3. Remove the bread when the toaster pops up, or when the bread is golden brown
    4. Allow the toast to cool for 2 minutes before proceeding
  2. Place the 2 slices of bread flat on a plate or cutting board
  3. Using a round-tipped knife, spread peanut butter on one slice of bread
  4. Wipe the knife off and use it to spread jelly on the other slice of bread
  5. Press the two slices of bread together
  6. Enjoy!
